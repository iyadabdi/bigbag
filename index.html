Everything you need

Built for the way
people actually shop

Most people shop across 5–10 different stores for a single haul. Big Bag was built for exactly that.

🛍
Save from anywhere

A floating "Save to Big Bag" button appears automatically on any product page. One click captures the title, price, image, selected variant, and link.

🏷
Smart grouping

View your bag grouped by store, product category (auto-detected), price range, or your own priority tags. Switch views instantly.

💰
Running total

See your estimated grand total across all stores and currencies, updated in real time. Know exactly what you're about to spend before you commit.

⚡
Buy All

One button opens each product page, automatically adds to cart, navigates to checkout, and autofills your saved shipping details.

✍
Checkout autofill

Save your name, address, and payment details once. Big Bag fills checkout forms automatically on any store — no retyping, ever.

🌙
Light & dark mode

A sleek dark mode with gold accents, and a crisp light mode with fuchsia accents. Both designed to feel fashion-forward, not generic.

How it works

Four steps to
a full haul

1
Install Big Bag

Add to Chrome in one click. No account, no sign-up, no email required.

2
Browse & save

Visit any product page. Click "Save to Big Bag" — it captures everything including your selected size and colour.

3
Review your bag

Open the popup to see all items grouped by store, category, or price. Adjust quantities, set priorities, see your total.

4
Buy all at once

Hit Buy All. Big Bag opens each store, adds to cart, goes to checkout, and fills in your details automatically.
